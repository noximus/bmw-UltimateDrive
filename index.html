The Ultimate Drive is now even more ultimate

Use The Ultimate Drive app to unlock a $300 mobile voucher toward a first lease or finance payment on an all-new 3 Series.
Exclusively for BMW Financial Services customers.
Available on the iPhone, iPad, iPod touch and Android devices.

How to earn $300

Step 1
Download The Ultimate Drive for your smartphone.
Available on the iPhone, iPad, iPod touch and Android devices.
Step 2
  • • Launch The Ultimate Drive.
  • • Tap DESIR3 a Test Drive.
  • • Schedule a test drive of the all-new 3 Series.
Step 3
  • • Complete your test drive.
  • • Enter your client advisor's name for confirmation.
  • • Claim your $300 Mobile Voucher.
*NOTE: Before generating the $300 Mobile Voucher, you will be asked to verify you are an existing BMW Financial Services customer by supplying your first and last name along with your billing zip code.
Step 4
  • • Give the voucher to your BMW Client Advisor when ordering your
       2012 3 Series before June 30, 2012.
